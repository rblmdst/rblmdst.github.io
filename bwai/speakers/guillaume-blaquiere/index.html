Innovation addict and Google Cloud 3x certified, writer and speaker in his free time, he's fascinated by the serverless solution and all the "usual" problems that it solves. More generally, he likes helping people stucks on Google Cloud; you can find him on Stack Overflow (guillaume-blaquiere), Medium (@guillaume-blaquiere) and Twitter (@gblaquiere)
